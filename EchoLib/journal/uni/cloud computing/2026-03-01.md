### Sunday, 01 March 2026
# Phase 1 ideas
**My project idea** -> movie/tv shows recommendations, catalog, review system (good reads for movies)

**description**: A cloud-native analytics and recommendation platform built on large-scale movie datasets
- main dataset - imbd dataset
	- https://developer.imdb.com/non-commercial-datasets/
	- https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/carolzhangdc/imdb-5000-movie-datasethttps://www.kaggle.com/datasets/carolzhangdc/imdb-5000-movie-dataset
- auxiliary datasets -
	- imbd reviews: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/lakshmi25npathi/imdb-dataset-of-50k-movie-reviews/data
	- tmdb 500 movies: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/tmdb/tmdb-movie-metadata
	- netflix prize award: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/netflix-inc/netflix-prize-data?select=movie_titles.csv
	- tmdb: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/asaniczka/tmdb-movies-dataset-2023-930k-movies
	- netflix (link to other platform dataset): https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/shivamb/netflix-shows
	- amazon prime: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/shivamb/amazon-prime-movies-and-tv-shows
	- disney+: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/shivamb/disney-movies-and-tv-shows
	- hulu: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/shivamb/hulu-movies-and-tv-shows


**Business capabilities** 
- provide searchable access to cleaned, structured movie/tv shows data (title, year, cast, genre, runtime)
- allow users to add ratings/reviews, enabling analytics around audience sentiment
- suggest movies to users based on their ratings history 
- Aggregate top‑rated, most popular or trending movies over time
- store personal watchlists for authenticated users
